Safety & Public Updates

Following the recent coaching centre fire incident in Lucknow, the Varanasi Development Authority (VDA) conducted a city-wide safety inspection across coaching institutes. During the drive, eight coaching centres were sealed for violating fire safety and building regulations. Authorities have directed educational institutions to improve emergency preparedness and strictly follow all safety standards to ensure student safety.

Varanasi News Highlights

Varanasi is witnessing rapid development with several major infrastructure projects, improved public transport, tourism initiatives, and smart city upgrades. Projects like the Ropeway, International Cricket Stadium, airport expansion, and railway redevelopment are transforming the city while preserving its rich cultural heritage.

Education & BHU

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) continues to strengthen its position as one of India's leading educational institutions through research programs, innovation centres, international collaborations, workshops, seminars, and student development activities. Several schools and colleges across Varanasi are also expanding their academic facilities.

Civic Development

Varanasi is undergoing one of its largest urban development phases with projects including the Ropeway Project, International Cricket Stadium, Kashi Railway Station Redevelopment, Airport Expansion, Transport Nagar, Smart Roads, Riverfront improvements, and modern water and sewerage infrastructure to improve public services.

Ropeway Cricket Stadium Airport Railway

Events & Industry

Varanasi regularly hosts cultural festivals, Ganga Aarti celebrations, trade fairs, startup events, business conferences, tourism programs, and spiritual gatherings. These events promote local industries, traditional crafts, handloom products, and strengthen the city's economy and tourism sector throughout the year.
